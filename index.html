About Me

I was Born in Miami, Florida with a latin background of PuertoRican and Nicaraguense but have always been a nerdy kid growing up, having interest in comics, games, and tech. My interests eventually lead me to persue an education in electronics which is what I got my associates on and there is where I was introduced to the world of programming. The first language I was exposed to was C/C++ and believe me it was not easy but that feeling I received after I had created something was amazing, it made me hungry to learn more. After graduating I then started teaching myself web developing, understanding the basics with HTML/CSS and then later joining a coding bootcamp hosted by the Univeristy of Miami. That experience opened the door to a whole wave of knowledge such as React, Node, databases and has left me thirsty as I understood this was only the tip of the iceberg. I currently continue my self teaching after finishing the bootcamp, you can never stop learning and have joined this meetup club by the name of "Decode Miami" teaching basic JavaScript to anyone that's interested(you'll be amazed how many seniors show up). I know that I can offer my commitment and full on dedication to any employer that will give me an opportunity, with my work experience I have gained the disciplinary mentality to achieve my goals and with that additude I plan on pushing any business to success.
